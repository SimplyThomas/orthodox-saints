## 3. What was built this phase

OS-0601–0700 spans the early Church, the Christological councils, Syrian and Palestinian ascetics,
the Slavic and Georgian monastic worlds, and the Russian/Byzantine hierarchy:

- **Gaza & Palestine** — the Gaza elders (Barsanuphius & John → Dorotheos → Dositheus); the
  Caesarea-Palestine martyrs (Pamphilus, Adrian, Eubulus, the virgin-martyrs); Martinian/Zoe/Photina.
- **Councils & hierarchs** — the **Chalcedon circle** (Leo the Great, Flavian, Anatolius, Pulcheria),
  the **Antiochene succession** (Meletius → Flavian, with Chrysostom), Photius the Great, and Pope
  Agathon (Sixth Council / Maximus).
- **Apostolic & early sees** — the Smyrna succession (Bucolus), Onesimus/Philemon/Archippus with
  Paul, Mariamne with Philip & Bartholomew, Auxibius of Cyprus with Mark.
- **Slavic & Balkan** — the **Nemanjić dynasty** (Symeon the Myrrh-gusher, Sava II), the Paroria
  hesychast circle (Theodosius & Euthymius of Trnovo), the Novgorod and Moscow hierarchs, the
  Vazheozersk Svir-disciples, and the Time-of-Troubles patriarch-martyrs (Hermogenes).
- **Georgia** — the Thirteen Syrian Fathers (Shio of Mgvime → 9 fathers), the Iqalto/Gelati academy
  lineage, and the Jerusalem Holy-Cross Georgian trio (Prochorus, Nikoloz Dvali, Luke).
- **Syria & Persia** — the Theodoret Syrian solitaries (Maron, Eusebius, Romanos), and the Persian
  martyrs under Shapur II (Sadoc ↔ Symeon of Persia).
- **British Isles** — the Lindisfarne/Iona succession (Aidan → Finan → Colman, with Cedd, Cuthbert).

## 5. Methodology & quality

Unchanged v1.3 pipeline. The conflation guard and adversarial judgment were especially productive
this phase: agents corrected several of my cluster hints (Rabulas postdates Theodoret; Agathon of
the Kiev Caves ≠ the Desert Father; Cornelius of Pskov ≠ the Centurion; Nikephoros the Leper ≠
Nikephoros of Chios) and surfaced four duplicate-pairs (Pancratius of Taormina, David of Gareji,
Anthony of Martqopi, Euphrosyne of Alexandria). One stray Theodoret card was removed centrally.
