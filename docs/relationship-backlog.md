# OrthodoxSaintFinder Relationship Backlog

Staging for relationship templates, groups, and deferred work. Process saints **in Saint-ID
order**; when a recurring pattern appears mid-batch, record it here and keep going.

## Promotion workflow (v1.3 — three tiers)
- **Candidate** — a recurring pattern worth watching; lives here. (No size requirement —
  historically significant small clusters are kept even if they never grow.)
- **Recommended** — well-established and ready; **recommend promotion** to
  `relationship-templates.md` on the owner's review.
- **Official** — promoted, living in `relationship-templates.md`. **Core** = the foundational
  subset (Holy Family, Twelve Apostles, Myrrh-bearers, Desert Fathers, Three Hierarchs,
  Cappadocian Fathers); change only on review.

Promotion to Official is applied only on the owner's review. *Recommended: none currently.*

Equal-to-the-Apostles · Mission to America / Alaska · Wonderworkers — all Stable→Official through
OS-0059 (now in `relationship-templates.md`).
