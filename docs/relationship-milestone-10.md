## 3. The first thousand — what was built (OS-0000 → OS-1000)

Ten phases of strict Saint-ID-order authoring, each a pair of 50-profile sub-batches fanned out to
five parallel subagents + central consolidation, every batch validated CLEAN. The network now spans:

- **Scriptural & apostolic** — the Twelve, the Seventy, the Myrrh-bearers, the Holy Family.
- **Patristic & conciliar** — the Cappadocian family, the Nicaea/Ephesus/Chalcedon/5th-Council
  circles, the Antiochene and Constantinopolitan successions, the Monothelite confessors.
- **Monastic** — the Desert Fathers (Egyptian, Judean, Sinai, Syrian-of-Theodoret), Mount Athos
  (Iviron, Philotheou, Dionysiou, Kavsokalyvia), the Kiev Caves, the Russian Northern Thebaid, the
  Optina & Roslavl elderships, the Gaza elders, the Thirteen Syrian Fathers of Georgia.
- **Martyric** — the Persian martyrs of Shapur II, the Martyrs-under-Julian, the Caesarea-Palestine
  and Gothic and Kazan and New-Martyr cohorts.
- **Western & Celtic** — the Lérins school, the Lindisfarne/Iona and Welsh and Irish circles, the
  Frankish missionaries, the Hispano-Roman siblings of Seville, the Italo-Greek south.
- **Dynastic households** — the Nemanjić, the Bagrationi, the Rurikid princes, the Branković.

## 5. Methodology & quality (the through-line)

The parallel-subagent + central-consolidation pipeline (frozen as v1.3 at the OS-0300 health review)
held across all ten phases: every batch validated CLEAN, every href resolved, no duplicate/self
cards survived the sweeps. The single highest-value quality lever proved to be **adversarial
subagent judgment** — across the thousand, agents repeatedly corrected cluster hints (this phase
alone: Avdas's persecution, two mis-identified confessors, a fool-for-Christ, a Maleon/Maleinos trap,
the Hypomone identity) and surfaced the duplicate pairs and false-negatives that the registers now
track. "Historical accuracy over symmetry" kept ~26% of profiles honestly sparse rather than padded.
