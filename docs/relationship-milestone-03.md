## 3. What was built this phase

OS-0201–0300 is dominated by **obscure pre-schism Western/Celtic local saints** (many correctly
sparse), large **New Martyrs of Russia** blocks, and several genuine hubs/clusters:

- **Boris & Gleb (OS-0236)** — Russia's first canonized saints; family reciprocity with Vladimir
  and Olga completed; archetype of a new **Passion-bearers** candidate.
- **Optina Elders** — Macarius of Optina (OS-0294) wired into the eldership cluster (Leonid,
  Ambrose, Moses, Anthony, Hilarion, Anatole I), reinforcing the existing `optina-elders` group.
- **Monastic federations:** Pachomian Koinonia (Petronius, Theodore the Sanctified, Horsiesios)
  and the Georgian David-Gareji circle — both Core-Desert-Fathers sub-clusters.
- **Byzantine Hymnographers** — Kassiani linked to Romanos, John of Damascus, Joseph, Theodore
  the Studite (new candidate).
- **Anti-persecution clusters:** Martyrs under Julian the Apostate; the Serbian WWII martyrs;
  the Lesbos and Lemko/Carpatho-Russian new-martyrs (the last two kept *distinct* from the
  Russian synaxis).
- **Apostolic & ecclesial links:** Evodius & Onesiphorus (Seventy: Peter, Ignatius, Paul);
  Macarius of Kanev / Athanasius of Brest / Job of Pochaev (anti-Union); the Hexham and Toul
  episcopal successions; Peresvet & Oslyabya ↔ Sergius of Radonezh.
- **Gregory's Dialogues** promoted Candidate → **Emerging** (recurring across three phases).

- **DI-002 — Edessa martyrs (OS-0190/OS-0191): Open** (needs synaxarion verification).
- **DI-003 — Rhais/Irais of Antinoe (OS-0226/OS-0779): Open** (probable duplicate).
- **DI-004 — Eupsychios of Caesarea (OS-0264/OS-1817): Open** (probable Hadrianic-pair duplicate;
  distinct from the Julian-era OS-0966).

All three open issues were surfaced *incidentally* by the relationship-authoring agents and
deliberately deferred — they point to a future dedicated duplicate-audit/cleanup pass.

## 5. Methodology

Each hundred ran as two 50-profile batches, each fanned out to **five parallel sub-range
subagents** under the frozen conventions, then a **central consolidation** (global
`href`-resolution verification, duplicate/self/name–note sweeps, group wiring, backlog/
data-issues updates, validation). The agents repeatedly demonstrated good adversarial judgment —
catching an incorrect cluster hint (OS-0246 is the Chonae shrine-custodian, not the Apostle
Archippus) and four distinct duplicate-saint pairs — confirming the parallel approach scales
without loosening the quality bar.
